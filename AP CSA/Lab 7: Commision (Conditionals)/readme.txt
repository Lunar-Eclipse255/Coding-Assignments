This program will determine what commission a salesman receives.  
If a salesman sells a car that is between $1000 and $10,000, they receive 10% of the sale.  
If the salesman sells a car that is less than or equal to $50,000 but more than previous condition, then the salesman receives 7.5% of the sale price.  
If the salesman sells a car that is greater than $50,000 then the salesman receives 5% of the sale.
